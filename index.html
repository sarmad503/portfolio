About Me

Turning Ideas Into
Visual Masterpieces

6+ Years of Excellence

I'm a passionate video editor and creative director with over 6 years of experience in the industry, having collaborated with hundreds of clients across the globe.

My journey has taken me through diverse projects - from high-energy sports content to emotional wedding films, dynamic music videos to compelling documentaries. I've had the privilege of working with professional cricket teams including Morrisville SAMP Army (Abu Dhabi T10), Larkana Challengers (SPL), and Chittagong Kings (BPL), managing their social media presence and creating engaging video content.

Beyond editing, I bring expertise in social media management, understanding how to craft content that resonates with audiences and drives engagement.
